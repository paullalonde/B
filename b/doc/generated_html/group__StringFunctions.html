const String &  str

related

Writes the characters of str to os. str is converted to ASCII prior to being written. If the conversion fails (eg there are non-7-bit-ASCII characters in str), an exception may be thrown.

If you need to write characters to a std::ostream that are in an encoding other than 7-bit ASCII, use String::Write() instead.

Reads the characters of the next word from is into the string str. The characters read from is are assumed to be 7-bit ASCII. If they aren't, an exception may be thrown.
